## Flag Thresholds
- Average < 3.0 -> `mentor_notify` -- very self-oriented leadership posture; mentorship conversation needed
- Item 6 (Putting Others First) score of 1 or 2 -> Note for facilitator

## Interpretation Notes
- Each item maps to a distinct servant leadership dimension from the SL-28 parent instrument.
- Growth tracking: Compare with ALQ (Session 14) and SPCS-R (Session 16) to track the leadership arc: servant orientation -> authentic practice -> influence readiness.
- Cross-reference with Session 1 (MLQ) vision statement -- servant leadership should align with the beneficiary's stated purpose.
