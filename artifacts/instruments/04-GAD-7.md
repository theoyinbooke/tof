# GAD-7 — Generalized Anxiety Disorder 7-Item Scale

## Response Scale
Prompt: "Over the last 2 weeks, how often have you been bothered by the following problems?"

| Value | Label |
|:---:|---|
| 0 | Not at all |
| 1 | Several days |
| 2 | More than half the days |
| 3 | Nearly every day |

## Items
| # | Item Text | Subscale | Reversed |
|:---:|---|---|:---:|
| 1 | Feeling nervous, anxious, or on edge | null | No |
| 2 | Not being able to stop or control worrying | null | No |
| 3 | Worrying too much about different things | null | No |
| 4 | Trouble relaxing | null | No |
| 5 | Being so restless that it is hard to sit still | null | No |
| 6 | Becoming easily annoyed or irritable | null | No |
| 7 | Feeling afraid, as if something awful might happen | null | No |

## Subscales
None. Single total score.

## Reverse Scoring
- Items: None
- Method: N/A

## Total Score
- Method: Sum of all 7 items
- Range: 0-21

## Severity Bands
| Range | Label | Flag | Platform Display Text |
|:---:|---|---|---|
| 0-4 | Minimal anxiety | none | "Your anxiety levels are minimal. Keep up the healthy practices you have in place." |
| 5-9 | Mild anxiety | none | "You are experiencing mild anxiety. The coping strategies from this session can help you manage these feelings." |
| 10-14 | Moderate anxiety | mentor_notify | "You are experiencing moderate anxiety. Consider discussing this with your mentor and exploring the coping tools from this session seriously." |
| 15-21 | Severe anxiety | admin_review | "Your anxiety levels are elevated. We strongly encourage you to speak with your mentor or a professional counselor. You are not alone in this." |

## Flag Thresholds
- Score 10-14 → `mentor_notify` — moderate anxiety warrants mentor check-in
- Score >= 15 → `admin_review` — severe anxiety requires professional attention

## Interpretation Notes
- The GAD-7 is a screening tool, not a diagnostic instrument. Scores >= 10 suggest clinically significant anxiety that warrants further evaluation. The platform should never provide a diagnosis — only indicate that further support may be helpful.
- Growth tracking: Re-administer at Session 9 (midpoint) and Session 16 (completion). Decreasing scores indicate the curriculum's emotional development components are working.
