# Session 1: See the Vision

## Overview
This is the anchor session of the entire curriculum. Every subsequent session references the vision statement written here. Beneficiaries discover God's purpose for their lives through biblical foundations, spiritual discernment, and practical vision-casting exercises.

## Key Topics
- Biblical foundations of vision (Habakkuk 2:2, Proverbs 29:18, Jeremiah 29:11)
- Spiritual discernment — learning to hear God's voice
- Identifying and overcoming vision blockers (fear, comparison, past failures)
- Writing a personal vision statement
- Aligning academic pursuits with God's calling

## Expected Outcomes
- Written personal vision statement
- Clarity on how current education connects to God's purpose
- 90-day action plan toward the vision

## Bridge Forward
Session 2 turns this vision into concrete, measurable goals.

## Mapped Assessment
- **Instrument:** Meaning in Life Questionnaire
- **Short Code:** MLQ
- **Items:** 10
- **Why:** Directly measures whether a person has clarity of purpose (Presence) and whether they are actively seeking it (Search). This maps perfectly to Session 1's goal of helping beneficiaries discern God's vision for their lives.
