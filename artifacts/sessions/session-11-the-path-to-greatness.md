# Session 11: The Path to Greatness

## Overview
Shifts from skills to character. Challenges cultural narratives about success by presenting Jesus' definition of greatness — servant leadership. Beneficiaries develop a personal leadership philosophy rooted in serving others.

## Key Topics
- Jesus' definition of greatness vs. the world's definition
- Servant leadership
- Character over credentials
- Finding your leadership voice
- Building legacy (connecting to Session 1 vision)

## Expected Outcomes
- Written personal leadership philosophy
- Leadership development plan
- Shift from self-advancement to community service

## Bridge Forward
Session 12 tests this leadership through adversity.
