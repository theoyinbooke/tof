# Session 4: Dealing with Anxiety

## Overview
Honestly acknowledges that trust and anxiety coexist. Provides practical tools alongside spiritual foundation. Beneficiaries learn to understand anxiety both biblically and psychologically, identify personal triggers, and build a resilience toolkit.

## Key Topics
- Understanding anxiety biblically and psychologically
- Identifying personal triggers and patterns
- Practical coping: breathing, grounding, cognitive restructuring
- When and how to seek professional help
- Emotional resilience as spiritual practice

## Expected Outcomes
- Personal anxiety management toolkit
- Ability to identify triggers before escalation
- Support network awareness
- Connection between emotional health and trust (Session 3)

## Bridge Forward
Session 5 deepens spiritual foundation through fasting and prayer.

## Mapped Assessment
- **Instrument:** Generalized Anxiety Disorder 7-Item Scale
- **Short Code:** GAD-7
- **Items:** 7
- **Why:** The gold standard screening tool for generalized anxiety. Brief, clinically validated, and widely used worldwide. Clear severity cutoffs enable flagging beneficiaries who may need professional support.
