# Session 10: Entrepreneurship

## Overview
Integrates Sessions 2 (goals), 6 (assets), 7 (financial management), and 9 (action). Beneficiaries develop an entrepreneurial mindset, validate business ideas, and learn the basics of launching a venture rooted in biblical principles of excellence, integrity, and service.

## Key Topics
- Entrepreneurial mindset vs. employee mindset
- Idea validation
- Business model basics
- Market research
- Launching with what you have (Session 6 callback)
- Biblical entrepreneurship: excellence, integrity, service

## Expected Outcomes
- Validated business idea or concept
- Basic business plan
- Revenue model understanding
- Connection to financial goals (Session 2)

## Bridge Forward
Session 11 asks what kind of person you are becoming in the process.

## Mapped Assessment
- **Instrument:** Entrepreneurial Self-Efficacy Scale
- **Short Code:** ESE
- **Items:** 23
- **Why:** Measures self-efficacy across five specific entrepreneurial competencies. Reveals exactly which areas of entrepreneurship a beneficiary feels confident in and which need development.
