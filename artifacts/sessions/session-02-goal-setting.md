# Session 2: Goal Setting

## Overview
Translates Session 1's vision statement into actionable SMART goals across all life areas. Beneficiaries learn to set biblically rooted, measurable goals and build accountability structures to sustain momentum.

## Key Topics
- From vision to goals: translating Session 1's output into targets
- SMART goals rooted in biblical wisdom
- Life area balance: spiritual, academic, financial, relational, and career goals
- Building a tracking system and overcoming procrastination
- Accountability structures

## Expected Outcomes
- Comprehensive goal framework covering all life areas
- 90-day action plan with weekly milestones
- Accountability partnership with a fellow beneficiary

## Bridge Forward
Session 3 addresses what happens when the path toward these goals feels uncertain.

## Mapped Assessment
- **Instrument:** Achievement Goal Questionnaire — Revised
- **Short Code:** AGQ-R
- **Items:** 12
- **Why:** Measures four types of goal orientation. Identifies whether a beneficiary approaches goals for growth (mastery-approach) or avoids them out of fear of failure (performance-avoidance). Directly reveals goal-setting posture.
