# Session 14: Authentic Leadership

## Overview
Advanced leadership development. Prepares beneficiaries for the reality of leading in workplaces and communities. Builds on servant leadership (Session 11) and resilience (Session 12) to develop leaders who lead from values under pressure.

## Key Topics
- Authentic vs. performative leadership
- Leadership identity
- Ethical decision-making
- Vulnerable leadership
- Building trust in professional settings (Session 3 callback)
- Leading diverse teams

## Expected Outcomes
- Written leadership identity statement
- Ethical decision-making framework
- Peer coaching relationships
- Connection to servant leadership (Session 11) and career readiness (Session 13)

## Bridge Forward
Session 15 sharpens the mind behind the leadership.
